Eggy Bread

Delicious sourdough bread whipped together with egg

This is a childhood staple of mine and has quickly become a staple of my own children's lunches.

It's tasty. It's nourishing. It's fluffy and soggy and exquisite. Fats, Carbs, Proteins all wrapped up in a delicious eggy bready package.


Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 slices of sourdough bread
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl
  2. Add salt & pepper to the egg mixture
  3. Heat the butter on the stove
  4. Soak one piece of bread in the egg bowl, making sure it seeps through
  5. Fry the bread on medium-high heat for about a minute on each side
  6. Plate the finished golden brown eggy bread and repeat with the two other pieces
