Banana Toast

Description

Delicious sourdough bread topped with honey & thin banana slices


Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • a pot of your favorite honey
  • butter (optional for extra creamy toast)
  • 1 banana

Steps

  1. Toast the bread till golden brown
  2. While bread is toasting, thinly slice the banana
  3. Spread toast with butter (optional)
  4. Spread the honey
  5. line the toast with thin slices of banana
  6. You can either leave the banana slices in tact or blend them in with the noney.
